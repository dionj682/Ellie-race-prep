Physical training
Form + strides
No new fitness builds in 5 days. What CAN improve is running economy — how efficiently your body moves. Drills and strides train this neurologically.
Easy jog
20 min conversational pace — RPE 4/10
If you can't hold a sentence, slow down
Drills
A-skips, B-skips, high knees, butt kicks — 2×20m each
Each drill isolates one part of the stride and trains efficiency
Strides
4×80m — smooth acceleration to race-feel, full rest between
Not a sprint. Rehearsing what race pace feels like so it's familiar Wednesday.
Cool-down
15 min stretch + foam roll — hips, hamstrings, calves

Cue word
A cue word is a mental trigger you can fire mid-race when things get hard. It needs to be loaded before Wednesday — which means practicing it now.
Evening
3 min cue word session — sit quietly, breathe slow, repeat your word while imagining running strong
Your word
Pick one that means "I'm ready." Examples: sharp · smooth · locked · mine · now
How
4 slow breaths. Say the word on each exhale. Picture yourself running strong at 600M.
By race day it becomes an automatic on-switch
Why
Your brain builds an association between the word and the feeling of running well. Wednesday you fire the word and the feeling follows.
